About

Wandering endlessly, walking aimlessly, getting lost and finding my way back, exploring places and impromptu trips – they bring me peace and joy. Often I am at loss for words to describe things I see and feel and this blog is an attempt to express them in the form of pictures.

I live in Amsterdam and I enjoy shooting Portraits, Architecture and Black & Whites. I hope you enjoyed my pictures as much as I did shooting them. Please don’t use my photos without permission.
